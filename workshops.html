Workshops

Based in the town of Arusha, each workshop begins with an introduction meeting where you will get to taste a selection of local beverages and get to know each other. We then travel to the chosen location by bus and begin the full workshop.
The workshops will guide you through:

  • Camera settings
  • Composition and Framing
  • Exposure and Colour settings
  • Distance for shots
  • Unique shots
  • Correct Lens choice

You will recieve feedback throughout the day and help on where to find your next destination. Each workshop has a maximum capacity of 10 but for larger groups please email for more information.

Prices start from $200.00 USD. To enquire on availability and to recieve a full guide on each workshop, hit the button below and fill in your details, you will recieve an email back within 24 hours.
